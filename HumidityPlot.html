Humidity vs. Latitude Analysis

Humidity and latitude seem to have some slight correlation, but it is not as easily observed as temperature. It looks like between 20 to 40 degrees latitude has the least humid cities, and -20 to -40 below the equator also appears to have the least humid cities. There is an interesting cluster of 90% humid cities at 60 degrees latitude.
