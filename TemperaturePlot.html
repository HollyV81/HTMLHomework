Max Temperature vs. Latitude Analysis

Temperature and latitude are highly correlated but temperatures North and South of the equator behave differently. Temperatures decline the further you get away from the equator both North and South, but they decline at a sharper rate North of the equator than they do South. Also, it is interesting that in my dataset the highest observed maximum temperatures occurred not on the equator but south of it.
